Description :

Nermin, a married woman, lives a forbidden love with her love. She makes a spell to be with her lover. But the consequences of this magic are very heavy. Because of the magic, Nermin's sister, Seher, is killed by intermission. Bedri, Nermin's hubby, is struggling with terrible nightmares every night. The most dangerous of genies is fighting Sinsiran.
